Hobbies

"Whatever you like to do, make it a hobby and whatever the world likes to do, make it a business..."

-Warren Buffett

Physical

Running

One of my foremost favorite hobbies to engage in during my freetime, as well as during Sport, is running. I do it as frequently as possible, and it helps clear my mind and in general improve my health.

Biking

Another physical hobby I like to do, particularly when the weather is good, is bike. Biking around with friends on local trails takes up a lot of my time during the summer, along with biking around as a form of commute. It improves your health, however, it first and foremost is fun which is the most important part out of all of this.

Weight-training.

Whilst I don't do it as much as some, I try to weight-train (with dumbells,) as much as possible. It is mainly an accessory to my sport~Track & XC~ but besides that it helps me improve my health, appearance, and generally is a good way for me to burn energy in my freetime.

Mental/Leisure

Reading

Reading is such a fun activity; despite some people decrying it as a waste of time and or boring. It helps me learn and understand on par with seeing it infront of me, and all around helps me become a more learned person; if that makes sense.

Cooking

I am not really that cooking, I have to come clean. However, it is one of my hobbies I enjoy a lot. Not because I could be good, or that I want to be good, but that I can work to get something I can enjoy.
