Clubs

Model UN

At Albert Grannis Lane Technical College Prepatory High School, there is a model united nations club. Model UN is an educational simulation where students role-play as diplomats representing UN member states to debate and solve global issues. I have been two two of these committees, or the model UN equivalent of a meet.

Sports Medicine

The Sports Medicine Club is a club in which, occasionally, you get speakers from a variety of sports medical related fields such as physical therapy and the like speak in regards to health. I joined it since, as an athlete, it helps me better understand my body and how to treat it properly.

Cartogrophy

Cartogrophy is a club which I joined since it represents an imaginative portion of me, a portion of me that can just think of other worlds. Joining Cartogrophy allowed me to put that to paper.
