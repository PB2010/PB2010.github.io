Beef-Barley Soup Recipe

The Beef Barley Soup originally related in Scotland as a broth, with it being made to include most nutrients and the like vital for survival.

Calories

Per serving, Beef-Barley Soup has around 100-150 calories.

Nutritional Benefits

It has lots of protein, fiber, iron, and essential vitamins.

Ingredients

  • Uncooked Barley
  • Sliced Onions
  • Sliced Carrots
  • Sliced Celery
  • Sliced Tomatoes
  • Garlic Knots
  • Basel
  • Sliced Potato Portions
  • Olive Oil
  • Pot of Water w/ cap.

Directions

Prepation

First and foremost, you cube the beef into 1x1 inch cubes. After this, start preparing the various fruits and vegetables cutting them into small portions and or dicing them if you would like smaller portions.

Cooking

Starting off, you put a pan on your stove and see to the browning of the beef cubes. Brown it in the olive oil for extra taste. After this, you bring a pot of water to a boil and toss the browned meet and the sliced/diced items in and begin stirring it, exempting basel and Garlic. Now, wait for two hours.

Second Period of Cooking

After the time, toss the basel and garlic in and turn down the cooking power to a lower level then cook for another two-ish hours. After this period you let it cool down, and boom you have your dish.
