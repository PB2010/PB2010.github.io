Barley-Beef Soup


Ingredients

  • Beef
    • Cubed,Well-browned
  • Barley
    • Uncooked
  • Sliced Onions
  • Sliced Carrots
  • Sliced Celery
  • Sliced Tomatoes
  • Garlic Knots
  • Basel
  • Sliced Potato Portions
  • Olive Oil
  • Lots of Water

Directions

  1. First and foremost, you cube the beef into 1x1 inch cubes. You should have a lot; not too much though as it is not the priority of your dish.
  2. After that; you put it in a pan and you put it on the stove. After this, you heat it up before tossing the beef cubes on it. Make sure its thoroughly browned.
  3. Whilst it is browning, start slicing the various things into small portions. Particularly, dice everything to extremely small cubes.
  4. Another pot should be filled with water and boiled to a point. Once boiling, toss the browned meat and the sliced goods in and begin stirring.
  5. Do NOT put in Basel, or Garlic as those will be added later for flavor.
  6. Wait about 2 hours. Cook at high temperature.
  7. After the two hours, put in the Basel and Garlic and cook low for another two hours.
  8. Let it cool down after finished, let cool down for like 20-30 minutes and enjoy.

History

The history of beef-barley soup is long and expansive, with it originating in Scotland as a broth of lots of different contents to allow for it to be easily made.
